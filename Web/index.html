OUR VALUES

Vision

Our vision is to enable efficient self-education for everyone. Traditional education methods are not always accessible or affordable, and self-education can be hard without guidance. We want to make it easy and effective for everyone to take control of their own learning and achieve their goals. Efficiency is key, so we aim to provide a user-friendly platform that maximizes learners's time.

Mission

The reason for the establishment of Unschooledd is to show people how a young team can educate themselves and each other, and how they can set up an enterprise from scratch, with the opportunities they have on the internet, as well as improving the experience of users with education. In this way, we try to show how self-learning and peer learning is possible!

What is self-learning?

Self-learning, also known as self-directed learning, is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, and abilities through personal initiative and without the guidance of a teacher or formal institution. In self-learning, individuals take responsibility for their own education, identifying what they need to learn and how they will go about doing so. This can involve a variety of resources, such as books, online courses, tutorials, and industry publications. Self-learning allows individuals to tailor their education to their specific needs and interests, enabling them to acquire the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in their chosen field.

Why is self-learning important?

Self-learning is an essential skill in today's fast-paced world. It enables individuals to take control of their learning experience and acquire knowledge on their own terms. The flexibility of self-learning is one of its primary advantages, allowing individuals to learn what they want, when they want, and at their preferred pace. This personalized approach enables learners to tailor their education to their specific interests and needs, making it especially beneficial for those with busy schedules or who cannot commit to traditional education due to other responsibilities. Moreover, self-learning helps individuals stay ahead of the curve by keeping them up-to-date with industry developments. In many fields, new technologies and methodologies are continually being introduced, and self-learners can adapt quickly to new trends, making them more competitive in the job market. Apart from these benefits, self-learning also boosts an individual's confidence and motivation. Taking control of their learning experience empowers individuals, leading to increased self-confidence and a greater sense of ownership and responsibility over their education. Self-learning gives individuals the power to shape their future and achieve their goals.
